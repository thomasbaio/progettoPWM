Welcome to SuperHeroes album community

Join a vibrant community of Marvel fans and explore everything about your favorite heroes! If you're curious about their stories, powers, or adventures — you've come to the right place.

What it is?

MV is a social group of people where you can create your card album, open card packages and trade your cards with other people.

here you can

  • Create and fill your card album.
  • Discover a vast selection of SuperHeroes.
  • Enjoy the thrill of opening card packages.
  • Connect with fellow enthusiasts and trade your cards.

Using Marvel APIs

MV integrates Marvel’s API to offer access to a vast collection of characters and related information. To dive deeper into Marvel’s API features, you can visit their official documentation. here.

About this project

This platform is built with Node.js and Express.js to ensure a powerful and scalable backend capable of managing complex operations with ease. It uses custom-built endpoints to enable smooth interaction between the server and the user interface,ensuring responsive and efficient functionality.
